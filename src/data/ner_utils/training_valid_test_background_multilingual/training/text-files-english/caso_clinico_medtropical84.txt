Clinical history A 48-year-old woman from Marandúa (Vichada-Colombia) presented with a 3-month history of an erythematous lesion in the right ear.
She had a history of trauma or insect bite.
He had been self-medicated with oral fluconazole, corticosteroids and topical antibiotics, with no improvement.
Physical examination revealed erythematous plaque, erythematous, nodular, palpable regional, non-painful, borderless, with some pustules on the helix and the right auricular concha.

Other complementary tests Direct examination and culture for Leishmania were negative.
Culture for deep mycosis in Sabouraud agar and potato and dextrose agar (PDA) after 10 days of isolation at 25°C was positive for Sporothrix schenckii.
