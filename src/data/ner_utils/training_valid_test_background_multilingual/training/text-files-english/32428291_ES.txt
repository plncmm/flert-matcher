Wuhan 74 year old man presented with fever (38 C), dry cough and tiredness.
A lung CT scan showed ground glass abnormalities.
The pharyngeal smear was positive for nucleic acid COVID-19.
The treatment consisted of hydroxychloroquine, lopinavir/ritonavir, thimosine and methylprednisolone.
A rash appeared on day 12.
On physical examination, the patient showed diffuse, irregular and partially confluent snoring throughout the body.
The patient was diagnosed with COVID-19 pneumonia and secondary urticaria of undetermined origin.
