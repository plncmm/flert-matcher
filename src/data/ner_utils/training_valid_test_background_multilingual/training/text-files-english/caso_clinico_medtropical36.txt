Male patient, 42 years old, reports the origin of the song Santa Elabes, today province of the same name and works in agriculture for various products, so he has frequent displacements, always within the coast.
He came to the service because of intense inflammatory erythema of the wings of the nose.
Scar in malar region
Total septum perforation was observed by ENT examination.
At the malar region there is a fibrous scar, 4 cm in diameter which, by reference to the patient, was an ulceration diagnosed as leishmaniasis, about 7 years ago.
The ulcer was not treated with any antineoplastic agent but only with "natural" or "ancestral" medicine of the site.
This infection was acquired while working in the "mountain" of Porto Alegre.
In the sample taken from the nasal mucosa it was observed facial expressions in the stained preparation and the RDI of leishmanin was positive 18 mm.
Treatment with trivalent antimalarial, Repodral, was satisfactory with healing, and the perforation persisted with permanent characteristics.
