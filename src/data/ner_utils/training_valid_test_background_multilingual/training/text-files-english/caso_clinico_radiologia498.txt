A 31-year-old male, Nigerian Emergency Department due to abdominal pain and vomiting.
There were no laboratory abnormalities in blood and urine.
Abdominal CT with contrast e.v.
Dilatation of some ileal and colon loops with liquid content and mild thickening of the terminal ileum wall of inflammatory aspect are observed.
Intracranial structures are identified in IDF, vermiformes, partially coiled.
With the suspicion of the presence of worms, abdominal ultrasound was performed curvilinear structures, with echogenic walls and hypoechoic tubular center compatible with Ascaris L parallel, confirming the presence of worms.
The patient was treated with 100mg ofbendazole every 12 hours for 3 days.
