JANF, 15 years old, male, from a community located in the KM-17 highway AM-010, municipality of Manaus (AM).
In August 2007, the patient presented fever, episodes of fever, dyspnea and chest pain.
The patient reported intake of açaí juice from the municipality of Coari in the same locality of the outbreak described above.
A diagnosis of ACE was made by positive thick blood smear for T. cruzi.
The electrocardiogram showed frequent ventricular extrasystole and the echocardiogram showed left ventricular dysfunction with an ejection fraction of 50%.
He was treated for HF with ACEI, beta-blockers and diuretics and an etiological treatment for CD was performed with▁benznidazole.
After the treatment, the examinations were normal and gradually tapered.
