An 80-year-old woman with anterior cervical pain started three days after FNAC on thyroid cyst.
Seven days later, the patient consulted due to worsening of the clinical picture, with dysphagia, fever and anterior cervical redness. The patient had started amoxicillin/clavulanic acid within 24 hours prior to admission (875/125).
PE: redness and swelling in the anterior cervical region.
Leukocytes 9,200 (79.3%N), VSG 73, Cr 1,26, VFG 40, PCR 296.6 ECO-PAAF LTI: cyst with two phases, anechogenic to milk aspirate phase is more liquid.
PA: sequestration, lymphocytes, neutrophils minimum material compatible with colloid.
Aerobic and anaerobic culture: negative.
Microorganisms were not observed in the GRAM stain.
The patient was treated with empirical broad-spectrum intravenous antibiotic therapy (meropenem 1 g/8h plus linezolid 600 mg/12h) for 14 days, continuing another 14 days orally (ciprofloxacin 500 mg/8h).
