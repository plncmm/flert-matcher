10-month-old male actant from Comayagüela, Francisco Morazán, with a history referred by the mother to present breathing difficulties and peripheral venous outpatient clinic hospital where she was referred to a university hospital.
According to clinical data obtained from the medical record, he was admitted to the pediatric emergency with a history of decay of one day of evolution, sudden abdominal pain, diarrhea of one day, on multiple occasions and vomiting of equal evolution.
On physical examination, the patient weighed 7.8 kg, heart rate was 30x min, respiratory rate was 65 x min, pulse rate was 30 x min, temperature was 36 oC, Glasgow 7/15, poor capillary secretion, mild reactive skin.
The patient was received in poor general condition, tachycardia, and cardiopulmonary resuscitation was performed for 30 minutes, without improvement, which was declared dead 55 minutes after admission.
The clinical diagnoses were acute diarrhea with 10% desaturation, hypovolemic shock and suspected intestinal perforation.
Legal medical autopsy was performed without significant external findings in the corpse, or in the macroscopic revision of the organs.
Microscopic examination revealed severe inflammatory infiltrate with the presence of lymphocytes, plasma cells, macrophages, and degeneration of the myocardium, some of which contained diffuse cardiac fibroids.
The lung presented mild edema, liver, spleen, intestine, brain and cerebellum showed vascular congestion.
According to the findings described, the diagnosis cause of death was acute myopathic arthritis and histology.
