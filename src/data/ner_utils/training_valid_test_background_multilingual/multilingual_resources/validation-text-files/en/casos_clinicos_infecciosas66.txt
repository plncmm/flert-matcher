Patient aged 48 years. No relevant medical history. He was hospitalised for septic shock due to COVID-19 respiratory focus. He started treatment with hydroxychloroquine + Tocilizumab (2 doses). Urinary sediment: waxy and granular casts 2/cpo + desquamative epithelial cells. Urinary sediment was interpreted as being attributable to ATN. He received daily extended haemodialfiltration. On day +12 of admission he recovered diuretic rhythm and improvement of azotemiated values.
