JRLD, 40 years old, male, from the city of Coari (AM). In April 2007, he presented with fever, anorexia and myalgia. Approximately during this period, an outbreak of RCT was identified with 25 cases in the municipality of Coari, probably related to the ingestion of açai juice; the patient reported having ingested açai from the same locality where the outbreak occurred, and required care in Manaus, where he was diagnosed with RCT due to a thick drop positive for T. cruzi. The electrocardiogram showed right bundle branch block and the echocardiogram was normal. After etiological treatment, the right bundle branch block disappeared.
