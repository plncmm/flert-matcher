An 8-year-old boy with asthma, sensitisation to Dermatophagoides and olive tree pollen and allergy to walnut and pistachio, presented with pharyngeal pruritus after drinking a glass of natural horchata (brand unknown), which comes from the Cyperus esculentus plant.

Results:
PP horchata Chufi® and OrXata!® negative. PP Tigernut PP negative. PPO: tolerates horchata Chufi®(UHT).
