During follow-up, despite adequate treatment and good management, a deterioration in lung function was observed, with a drop in FEV1 of the patient. An extensive study was conducted to rule out comorbidities (Pr. 11% bacterial test, skin culture).
The results obtained showed total IgE of 1713 U/ml, specific IgE to Aspergilus and positive precipitins, sputum culture and positive skin test to Aspergilus Fumigatus antifungal treatment, confirming the diagnosis of Aspergillus.
