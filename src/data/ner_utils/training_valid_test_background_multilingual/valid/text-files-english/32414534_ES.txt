A 69-year-old man with fever and cough for 7 days was admitted to the University Hospital of Guadalupe (French Indians) with disorientation and severe headache.
She had no history and reported traveling to the Middle East (crucery vacation) with her wife 15 days before hospitalization.
He did not report insect bites.
One week after she returned home, she had fever, myalgia, cough, anosmia, ageus, cervical pain, stiff neck and diarrhea.
On admission, she reported worsening of her condition in the last 24 hours, with stiffness and neck pain, severe headache, disorientation, inability to walk, with falls, and dyspnea.
It should be noted that his wife had also suffered cough and apathy for the past 10 days.
