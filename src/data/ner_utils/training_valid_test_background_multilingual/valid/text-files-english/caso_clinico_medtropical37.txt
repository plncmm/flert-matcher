This is a woman, aged 25 to 30 years, from the area located between San Carlos, Quevedo and Quinsaloma, provinces of Los Rios and Bolivar, in a tropical forest located in the state of Bolivia.
He has a total perforation of the nasal septum, a great erythema extending to the edge of the wings of the nose.
The ENT examination does not show lesions in other sectors.
The skin examination revealed an indelible scar on the right side of a diameter of 6 cm, of an ulceration that had been diagnosed six years ago, had no etiologic diagnosis and he was treated with traditional treatment.
The smear test, stained with Wright, did not demonstrate the presence of Leishmania sp.
and the DRI of leishmanin measured 24 mm, so treatment was started with 20 ampoules of glucantime, 1 daily IM.
Healing was achieved at the end of treatment and in the 4-month control, internal foramen was observed but no signs of activity.
The patient continues to live in the same place of origin.
