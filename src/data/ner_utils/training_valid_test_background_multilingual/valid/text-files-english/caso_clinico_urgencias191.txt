A 75-year-old patient with a history of TBC, BPH and bilateral cataract underwent right hemicolectomy with placement of mesh and umbilical hernia repair. She was admitted to the intensive care unit and underwent surgical drainage of the mesh.
Results: The evolution of the patient was satisfactory, achieving resolution of the seroma in one week, allowing healing every 48 hours, with better quality of life for the patient.
And the curing of infected mesh becomes a plant with continuity with NPT.
